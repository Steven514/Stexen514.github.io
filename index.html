Letter Lancers is a 4-player couch co-op bullet-heaven horde game that will have you and your friends laughing your way through epic battles!

Grab your friends, pick your characters, and get ready to ruin someone's day! Level up, select from hundreds of modifiers, get loot, and earn your place on the leaderboards!

  • Play alone, or play with your friends!
  • Local co-op up to 4 players. Get some buddies together in person or invite your Steam friends to play with via Steam's Steam Remote feature.
  • Players are rewarded more while playing with others with faster unlocks and more meta progression currency.
  • 1 of multiple game modes -- The standard way the game was meant to be played.
  • Dungeon crawl through procedurally generated rooms, kill all enemies, level up, and get that sweet, SWEET, loot!
  • Struggling with your run and need a boost? We got you. Get rewarded at the end of each run, even if you fail. Rewards can unlock meta progression upgrades to make your runs easier. You got this buddy.
  • A different game mode featuring it's own 24 hour leaderboards. Top daily scores are posted in the Letter Lancers discord server.
  • A procedurally generated ruleset our servers generate that changes the gameplay experience everyday, adding different gameplay modifiers, allowing for only certain characters to be used, mixes up enemies spawned, and ether increases the player's power scaling fantasy, or gives you a reallllll hard time.
  • Compete in leaderboards specific to level, amount of players, and game mode.
  • Daily leaderboards reset every 24 hours, and top scores are posted and immortalized in the Letter Lancers discord.
  • Create different builds from hundreds of modifiers.
  • 40+ weapons.
  • 24+ playable characters.
  • 70+ meta progression upgrades to help you on your standard runs
  • 2 different game modes, 1 of which offers players a different experience every single day.
  • More to come!
